Say Hello to
Inboxd

Inboxd is an AI-powered email assistant designed to make professional communication effortless. It reads your incoming messages, understands your intentions, and generates context-accurate replies, saving you time while keeping your tone clear and appropriate. Whether you need to respond quickly on the go or draft polished messages for work, Inboxd helps you stay organized, prompt, and confident in every conversation.

Inboxd's Top Features

AI Adaptability tailors each email to the context of your email and style of your writing, all while considering your personal preferences regarding the tone of the email.

Easy user access is promoted by an easy-use design and offering quick-access to your favourite themes and tones to maximize email response efficiency.

Write only 20 characters and Inboxd fully understands your intentions, tone, and relationship with the recipient, personalizing an email for you!

About Inboxd

Inboxd was founded by two passionate high school seniors tired of watching the rest of the world adapt and invent whilst communications were left sub-par.

Although AI email assistants did exist, their design, user interface, and capabilities let their consumers down. Thus, they founded Inboxd: A revolutionary extension, app, and email service to make communication all-the-more worthwhile and effortless.
